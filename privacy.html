Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 6, 2025

1. Information We Do Not Collect

By default, StudyLock does not collect any personal or non-personal information. Users can use the app and all its core features without providing any identifiable data.

2. Optional Data Collection for Study Groups

If you choose to join a Study Group, we may collect your email address through Google or Microsoft sign-in. This is used solely for group functionality, such as verifying access and syncing your study group status. We do not use this data for tracking, advertising, or analytics.

3. Use of Information

The email address collected (if any) is used exclusively to enable optional Study Group features. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with any third parties.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share or disclose any user information unless legally required to do so. Study Group email data is stored securely and only accessible to maintain functionality.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any user data related to Study Groups. While no app can guarantee absolute security, your privacy and data integrity are important to us.

6. Data Retention

We retain your Study Group email only as long as necessary for group functionality. You can request removal at any time by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. All changes will be posted on this page. We encourage you to review it periodically. Continued use of StudyLock constitutes acceptance of the latest version.

8. Your Consent

By using StudyLock, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you join a Study Group, you consent to limited data collection as described above.
