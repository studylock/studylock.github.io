FAQ

Does StudyLock work on iPhone and iPad?

Yes — StudyLock is built for iOS and works on iPhone and iPad (iOS 16+ recommended).

Can I choose which apps to block?

Absolutely. Pick any installed apps to block while you study. They’ll unlock only after you pass your quiz.

Do you store my documents?

Your privacy matters. Documents are used to generate summaries and quizzes; see our Privacy Policy for details.

Is there a free version?

Yes. You can download and start for free, then upgrade if you want more power features.
